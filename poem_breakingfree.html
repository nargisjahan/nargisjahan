Breaking Free

The wings of ambition flap hard
Urging me to fly out into the unknown
While swirling mists and a fog of hesitant doubt
Clog my consciousness making me dizzy

And I try to find my position and decipher my aim
As I yearn to know what liberation feels like
I then invest all my energy into breaking the manacles
Determined to free the zealous wrist

Which I with all my heart believe
Will guide me to my destination
Or so I hope.
