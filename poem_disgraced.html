Disgraced

Life shall never be the same again
Cleansed of joy it is now full of pain
No efforts can wipe away the stain
And the bruise won’t let me be sane.

Miss the infectious smile I would adorn
Caring not a bit for any criticizing scorn
Enthusiasm that would fill me every morn
But this while I feel dejected, weak and torn.

Life doesn’t seem beautiful any more
Going through every mundane daily chore
It appears destiny has closed each door
Leaving me in a wretched self pitying stupor.

Forever shall the cruel Disgrace stay
Ruining every single hour of my day
Dropping thorns to obstruct my way
For which sin am I being made to pay?

Carnage looks back from every sight
Darkness deep in open sunlight
The memory threatens in dreams at night
As I recoil and scream with increasing fright.

The course of tears I slowly trace
A broken soul, I badly need solace
I plead to give me back my grace
To put the smile back in its old place.
