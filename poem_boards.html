The Nightmare Called Boards

Under tremendous stress and strain
Wailed every inch of my brain
To seek some succour some relief
From the maddening days of grief

In trying to swallow the impossible pile
Of material on the desk while
The scared heart let out a pitiful moan
And a wretched me began to groan
And pray for deliverance from this pain.
I tried to focus and be a little sane

Whenever I felt like I would swoon
I would say ‘this too shall pass’ and soon.
Well now it has passed, I’m a free soul

Relieved to discover I’m hale and whole
After all the dents and bruises and cuts
The boiling tension that drove me nuts
Brought me often to the verge of tears
As I struggled against my deepest fears

Now after weeks of daunting despair
I gladly throw away every ‘petty’ care
Put behind all those memories vile
Summon my lost long ago smile

And nose dive into the world of fun
For the evil boards are finally done!
